MISSION:

Feed and clothe the needy of Louisville, KY and Southern Indiana

"For I was an hungered, and ye gave me meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me drink: I was a stranger, and ye took me in: Naked, and ye clothed me: I was sick, and ye visited me: I was in prison, and ye came unto me." Matthew 25:35-36

ABOUT US

Feed the City is a successful non-profit organization providing many community programs that has been serving the city since 2006.

We offer daily hot meals and emergency food boxes to everyone.

The clothes closet is available for those in need of clothing.
