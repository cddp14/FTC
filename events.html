Summer Food Service Program(SFSP) 2016

SFPS will serve food to kids in low-income apartment complexes during summer break. Many families depend on school lunches to help feed their kids,therefore, SFSP has stepped in to ensure no child goes hungry during the summer months. This is a USDA program* being sponsered by Feed the City.
