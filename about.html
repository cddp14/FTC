About Us

Feed the City is a successful non-profit organization providing many community programs and has been serving the city since 2006.

We offer daily hot meals and emergency food boxes to everyone.

The clothes closet is available for those in need of clothing.

Executive Director

Pastor Larry D. Coleman attended Southern Baptist Boyce Bible College for four years. He severed on the board of Prodigal Ministries as Program Coordinator; he also served on staff as an ordained Minister with the Evangel World Prayer. Pastor Coleman was the Executive Director of the Lords Kitchen and founded the prison ministry called Reconciliation Ministries. He is also the Founder and President of City Church Inc. Pastor Coleman is devoted to his wife and children.
